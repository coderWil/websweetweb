About Me

I'm a freelance web developer who believes great software sits at the intersection of clean code and thoughtful user experience. With a background spanning both front-end and back-end development, I focus on building applications that are not only functional but genuinely enjoyable to use. Every project starts with understanding the person on the other side of the screen.

Over the years I've worked with startups and established businesses alike, helping them turn ideas into polished, production-ready products. I care deeply about performance, accessibility, and maintainable architecture — the kind of details users may never notice, but always feel. When I'm not coding, I'm usually exploring new frameworks or contributing to open-source projects.
